TRI-FOLD BROCHURE

This tri-fold brochure was designed for a mock travel campaign for Aegean Airlines. The layout contains information, travel tips, and popular things to do in Santorini, Greece. This project was made as an assignment for the UM course 'STC102 - Graphic Design for Strategic Communication.'

October 22nd, 2023
