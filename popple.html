POPPLE MAGAZINE

I designed a 10 spread magazine titled POPPLE that features a variety of articles exploring the landscape of women in pop music. This was a really fun and creative project to work on, as it allowed me to play around with details and colors to give a unique look to every page. This project was made as an assignment for the UM course 'STC102 - Graphic Design for Strategic Communication.'

DATE:

December 19th, 2023

PROGRAMS USED:

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop
