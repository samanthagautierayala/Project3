TYPE SPECIMEN BOOK

I created a 5-spread type specimen book for the font 'Didot.' I decided to select a 'casino' theme for the design of the pages and the overall aesthetic of the book. This project was made as an assignment for the UM course 'STC202 - Advanced Graphic Design for Advertising.'
